More About Kam Musika Hospital

KAM MUSIKA HOSPITAL is registered under KAM DSM PHARMACY LIMITEDCompany, this company was found and registered under the law of United Republic of Tanzania companies Act of 2002 in the year 2004 with registration number 48694. The headquarters of the company is at KimaraKorogwe, Ubungo Municipal Dar-es-Salaam Tanzania, where the hospital is located too. KAM Musika Hospital complies with the Ministry of Health and Social welfare guidelines that allows the Hospital to operate at District level.

The best part of KAM Musika Hospital includes its ability to cope with upward and changing demand which is operational in several factors mainly competitive, economic, social and technological improvement as far as healthcare services are concerned.

The hospital have enough qualified and experienced health workers including Doctors, trained Nurses, Specialists, Technical staffs, Administrative staffs and supportive staffs to support the company activities. We have a total of enough numbers of employees who works 24/7 to make sure our patients get services of quality and at the right time. Also is equipped with the modern facilities which simplify the work of service providers and patient feel better when getting services.

The services offered at the hospital includes Maternity, Nursing, Surgery, Pharmacy, Diagnosis/Preparation, Emergency Medicine, Internal Medicine, Anesthesiologists, Dermatology, Otolaryngology, Diagnostic Radiology, Medical Genetics, Neurology, Ophthalmology, Pathology, Psychiatry, Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, Pediatrics, Preventive Medicine, Radiation Oncology, Urology, Orthopedic Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology.

The Hospital has a role to play in the development of the nation as a whole, and seeks to play its rightful role in the maintenance of peoples` health.
